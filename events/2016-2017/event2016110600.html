Dear members,

Cambridge's biggest Asian clubbing night is BACK!

Just as the memories of last year’s Funkyard seemingly fade away, that routine post-Halloween tingling sensation tells me otherwise. For once a year, ABACUS acts as the connector of dots, bringing together every Asian society in Cambridge for one HUGE November extravaganza. Ladies and Gentleman, we are proud to announce that Funkyard is back!

Joining us this year, we have:
CU Chinese Society
CU Chinese Cultural Society
CU Korean Society
CU Anglo-Japanese Society
CU Malaysia Society
CU Malaysia and Singapore Association
CU East Asian Cultural Society
CU India Society
CU Taiwanese Society

IT’S BACK, BETTER AND BIGGER THAN EVER!!

Having booked out a club exclusively for this event, we are extremely proud to announce that we are hosting this year’s Funkyard at Revolution, a modern club with a proper music license and higher ceilings than Kuda and Cindies combined. We also have Cambridge's very own Matt Peniket to mix up some tunes, so make sure to bring your dancing shoes and be ready to cut some serious shapes!

Get hyped and prepare yourself for an unforgettable night!!! When the night is still young, the party never dies.
