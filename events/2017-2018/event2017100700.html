Freshers' Squash


Dear our fresher friends,

Come along to our Freshers’ Squash to get involved in one of the hottest societies in Cambridge!! The Cambridge University Korean Society (CUKS) welcomes everyone who has interests in Korean culture / language / food & drinks.

When?

Saturday 7th October – 19:00-23:00

Where?

Clare college (Garden Room / Riley Auditorium)

What?

CUKS sign up, Free pizza, snacks and drink with a brief introduction from the president about what CUKS is, and what events we’ll be holding over the coming year, check out our Freshers’ Guide, CUKS donations Raffle, CUKS logo election, blood sibling project

Membership fee?
Pay at the squash: (Cash Only) £15 including VAT and one raffle ticket
Squash raffle ticket: £3 per ticket

Freshers' squash timetable

Before 7pm : Pre-drink by your own
7pm ~ 8:00pm : Champagne & Pizza
8pm ~ 8:40pm : Presentation from committee
8:40pm ~ 9pm : Blood brothers & family event
9pm ~ 9:20pm : Raffle event
9:20pm ~ 11pm : Wine & Snacks
