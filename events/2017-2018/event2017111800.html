Oxbridge Boat Party


Celebrate under the stars at the biggest Oxbridge party of the year.

For one night only, cruise along the Thames on a luxury party yacht in the annual OUAPS & ABACUS Cambridge flagship party. Join us in the City of Stars, for an unforgettable evening of extravagance and celebration.

On this yacht, there’s something for everyone. Three decks of entertainment await, they’ll never be a dull moment. For the first few hours you can learn to waltz on the dance floor, and after that out comes the DJ so you can party the night away. Elsewhere indoors there’s a casino so you can win big, henna and glitter painting so you can look your absolute best, and fortune teller who can tell you what lies ahead. To help you immortalise your memory of the night there’s a photobooth indoors, and a photo scene outdoors. To help you along all guests get a glass of wine, canapes, and – to top it off – there’s two chocolate fountains.

Meet new friends as hundreds of students travel across the country for the party of a lifetime. We look forward to seeing you at City of Stars, the biggest Oxbridge party of the year!

Ticket sales close on the 17th at 5:30 pm

Date: Saturday, 18 November 2017
Time: 7.30 PM
Location: Tower Millenium Pier, London
