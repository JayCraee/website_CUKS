2019 Annual General Meeting


Dear Members of CUKS,

Our biggest event of the year the Annual Dinner and Annual General Meeting will take place on Thursday, February 28th, 2019 at Robinson College. Join us in this meaningful event in which the 2019/20 committee members of CUKS will be elected. It will be a great chance for you to catch up with your fellow CUKS friends as well. Please note that spaces are limited for this event and only 50 seats are available, first come first served.


The schedule for the evening is as follows:

6:30pm – 7:00pm: Reception drinks (quality sparkling wine will be served)
7:00pm – 9:00pm: Annual Dinner
9:00pm – 10:00pm: Annual General Meeting


1. Annual Dinner

Please note that spaces are limited for this event and only 50 seats are available, first come first served. If you wish to attend the event, please sign up using the following link:

https://joinagora.com/events/26816/?view=public#tickets

and secure your space by paying £24.95(member)/£30.95(Guests). This price includes a delicious three course meal and wine.


The menu for this formal will be as follows:

Starter:
Soup, freshly made by the Chef

Main Dish:
Non-Vegetarian:
Brochette of beef rump marinated in garlic, English mustard and thyme
Vegetarian:
Baked Portobello mushroom and egg Florentine

Dessert:
Chocolate pistachio layer tart

Please understand that your place will only be guaranteed once your payment is complete. We look forward to meeting you at the dinner.


2. Annual General Meeting

It's time to elect the next members of the committee!

This year's AGM will take place on Thursday, February 28th 2019, following the Annual Dinner. Please join us to elect the next committee members. It is also an opportunity to present your views on how the society should be run and show your support for the incoming committee.

The following is the list of committee roles. For those who wish to apply, please check the <Committee Roles for CUKS 2019-2020> document for further details on the committee roles and application process.

  • - President
  • - Undergraduate Vice-President (Financial)
  • - Undergraduate Vice-President (Operational)
  • - Public Relations (PR) Officer
  • - Social Media & Communications Officer
  • - Information Technology Officer
  • - Secretary
  • - Freshers' Rep

We hope many of you are interested in joining the incoming committee. If you have any queries regarding the committee roles, application process or the AGM, please do not hesitate to contact us.
